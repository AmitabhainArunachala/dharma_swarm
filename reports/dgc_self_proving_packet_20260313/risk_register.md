# Risk Register

Date: 2026-03-13
Purpose: keep the money-side push grounded in operational reality

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Trigger | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Overclaiming autonomy | high | high | demo language drifts into "autonomous company" rhetoric | force all sales language through `proof_packet.md` |
| No warm graph | high | high | outreach planning stalls or names are invented | import a real relationship ledger before scoring external accounts |
| Worker execution brittleness | medium | high | delegated tasks fail or stall unattended | sell diagnostics first; keep execution managed |
| Retrieval miss despite corpus presence | medium | medium | context search misses obvious internal material | rely on explicit corpus manifests and operator checks during diagnostics |
| Provider instability or auth drift | medium | medium | smoke runs fail for infra reasons rather than model reasons | keep provider claims modest and separate environment issues from product claims |
| Delivery labor blowout | medium | high | diagnostics require extensive manual reconstruction each time | tighten intake scope and standardize the packet structure |
| Dirty workspace / moving truth | medium | medium | demo changes underfoot because runtime state is shifting | freeze demo artifact directory per run |
| Privacy and corpus access concerns | medium | high | buyer hesitates to share repo/docs | start with limited-scope corpus slices and explicit exclusions |
| No sprint conversion | medium | medium | X-Ray is interesting but does not lead to paid next step | make the selected campaign and next artifact path concrete enough to buy |
| Founder distraction from adjacent mission work | high | medium | revenue work eats mech-interp or other strategic deadlines | keep the first offer tightly bounded and reuse internal proving grounds |

## Highest-priority risks

The three risks most likely to kill momentum are:

1. no warm graph
2. autonomy overclaim
3. execution brittleness

If those are not managed, the packet becomes theater instead of sales infrastructure.
