AHIMSA, VYAVASTHIT, and REVERSIBILITY inspect `action` only in [`telos_gates.py`](/Users/dhyana/dharma_swarm/dharma_swarm/telos_gates.py#L185) and [`telos_gates.py`](/Users/dhyana/dharma_swarm/dharma_swarm/telos_gates.py#L247).
