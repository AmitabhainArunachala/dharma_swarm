# Strategic Partnership Follow-up Email

## Recommended Use

Send this only after the Research Awards submission email has been sent. The draft is intentionally short and low-friction: it adds useful context, makes no claim of institutional affiliation, and asks Anthropic to route the conversation if there is a better lane for complementary tooling or data support.

## Email

**To**: economicfutures@anthropic.com

**Subject**: Follow-up on Economic Futures submission: Welfare-Tons research proposal

Hello Anthropic Economic Futures team,

I recently submitted the Welfare-Tons proposal, "Measuring Labor Market Outcomes of Corporate Carbon Investment: Welfare-Tons as a Joint Workforce-Environmental Impact Metric," to the Economic Futures Research Awards track and wanted to share one brief follow-up note.

I am an independent researcher and solo builder, not a research institution, so I am not trying to frame this as a formal strategic-partnership application. The reason for the note is that, alongside the proposal itself, I have built an auditable research workflow in `dharma_swarm` for reproducible analysis, provenance tracking, and rapid synthesis around labor-transition and sustainability questions. If useful, I would be glad to send a short technical note on how that tooling could support adjacent Economic Futures work, including Economic Index analysis or pilot studies tied to community-impact measurement.

If the right path is simply to keep everything within the Research Awards submission, that is completely fine. If there is a better lane for an independent researcher to share complementary tooling or discuss API or data support, I would appreciate guidance.

Best,

John Shrader
