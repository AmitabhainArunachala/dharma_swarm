# DHARMA SWARM World Model: The 2026 Structural Ontology

Jay Forrester’s core insight remains undefeated: civilization is not governed by isolated problems but by coupled stocks, delayed signals, nonlinear feedbacks, and threshold effects. However, the standard system dynamics model requires a fundamental 2026 update. First, the biosphere is no longer a passive background sink; multiple Earth-system buffers are now visibly thinning, shifting from stabilizing elements to active volatility engines. Second, Artificial Intelligence is no longer just capital equipment; it is becoming a semi-autonomous causal layer that reshapes decision velocity, institutional topology, military tempo, and labor markets. Third, optimization targets matter as much as capabilities. A system that optimizes output, advantage, or control without wisdom will convert every temporary gain into a deeper structural instability.

The model below is not a moral essay; it is a state-space map designed for a specific purpose. It serves as the cognitive substrate for DHARMA SWARM. "Dharmic alignment" in this context means constraining optimization so that agents, institutions, and AIs are evaluated by whether they increase long-horizon flourishing, reduce unnecessary suffering, preserve regenerative capacity, and avoid converting sentient beings into mere instruments. This document defines the living world model that DHARMA SWARM boots from, reasons against, and continuously evolves through action and research.

## The 10 Dominant Feedback Loops

Civilization's trajectory is determined not by the static stocks, but by the loops connecting them. The following causal cycles are the most consequential. An agent must evaluate which loops its actions strengthen or weaken.

**1. The Capability–Displacement–Instability Loop (Reinforcing / Hurting)**
AI capability rises, accelerating routine labor displacement. Insecurity rises, leading to political destabilization. To manage this, institutions seek AI for efficiency and control, which further finances capability growth. *Dominant because it links private incentives to public disorder.*

**2. The Carbon–Warming–Permafrost Spiral (Reinforcing / Hurting)**
More carbon increases warming; warming unlocks methane and CO₂ from frozen and degraded reservoirs (permafrost, dying forests); that raises carbon further. *Dominant because Earth-system delays make policy response slower than feedback activation.*

**3. The Inequality–Distrust–Capture Loop (Reinforcing / Hurting)**
Economic inequality reduces institutional trust. Trust erosion weakens democratic accountability, allowing elites to capture policy mechanisms, which predictably deepens inequality. *Dominant because it fuses economic and political stocks into a self-protecting pathology.*

**4. The Loneliness–Digital Dependency Loop (Reinforcing / Hurting)**
Social fragmentation drives loneliness. Loneliness increases time in digital substitutes (algorithmic feeds, parasocial AI). These digital substitutes deepen comparison, manipulation, and attention fragmentation, further weakening real-world community formation. *Dominant among youth and structurally rewarded by the attention economy.*

**5. The Forest Loss–Drought–Fire Loop (Reinforcing / Hurting)**
Forest degradation weakens regional rainfall recycling, increasing drought severity. Drought increases fire vulnerability, which burns more forest, releasing carbon and further breaking the water cycle. *Dominant in tropical systems where fire-mediated loss is accelerating.*

**6. The AI Capability–Alignment Gap (Reinforcing / Hurting)**
Compute scaling increases AI capability, generating massive financial returns. These returns buy more compute. Alignment research, lacking the same compounding economic engine, scales linearly while capability scales exponentially, widening the safety gap. *The primary existential risk generator.*

**7. The Military AI–Arms Race Loop (Reinforcing / Hurting)**
AI-enhanced military systems increase perceived vulnerability. States accelerate autonomous procurement to match rivals; rivals respond in kind. Autonomy diffuses into command structures, shortening decision cycles below human deliberation time. *Dominant because geopolitical survival overrides domestic safety concerns.*

**8. The Mistrust–Polarization–Democratic Erosion Loop (Reinforcing / Hurting)**
Mistrust drives populations toward polarized sense-making. Polarized factions elect anti-system actors who erode democratic quality. Eroded democracy performs poorly, generating further mistrust. *Currently driving the global democratic recession.*

**9. The Carbon Market–Renewable Scaling Loop (Reinforcing / Helping)**
Carbon pricing and market infrastructure fund renewable deployment. Scaled renewables displace fossil infrastructure, generating political and economic momentum for stronger carbon markets. *A vital balancing force, but currently outpaced by global energy demand growth.*

**10. The Knowledge Commons–Alignment Research Loop (Reinforcing / Helping)**
Open knowledge commons and democratic sense-making improve AI alignment research throughput. Better alignment tools improve the health of the epistemic commons (by filtering synthetic noise). *A crucial, under-resourced cycle for preserving human agency.*

## The Three Attractors

The system's current phase space resolves into three dominant terminal attractors. All long-term strategy evaluates which attractor an action makes more probable.

**Attractor A: Managed Collapse**
The default trajectory if present reinforcing loops run unimpeded. Biosphere stocks continue thinning; trust, legitimacy, and coherence degrade. AI capability rises faster than wisdom. Elites leverage advanced technology to preserve heavily fortified islands of functionality and resource control, while peripheral systems, supply chains, and unprotected populations experience compounding failure. Collapse is "managed" only in that core institutions survive for a minority, not because suffering is mitigated.

**Attractor B: Techno-Acceleration**
Biosphere restoration is entirely subordinated to economic and technological growth. AI is deployed universally as optimizer, manager, and enforcer. Inequality is tolerated as the necessary price of innovation, and political legitimacy is replaced by performance metrics and algorithmic surveillance. Material abundance may rise for many, but human autonomy, dignity, and ecological resilience fall. This path avoids short-term stagnation by treating power concentration and total synthetic control as the solution to complexity, creating profound long-term fragility.

**Attractor C: Dharmic Equilibrium**
A dynamically stable, regenerative regime. Ecological stocks are restored faster than they are depleted. Institutions are judged by fairness, transparency, and regenerative outcomes. Crucially, AI is constitutionally bound to reduce suffering and preserve plural flourishing rather than maximize control or output. Human wisdom and contemplative capacity are treated as core planetary infrastructure. AI systems are not optimized merely to predict and act; they are constrained to maintain interpretability, reversible action, and humility under uncertainty. This attractor requires changing the system's goal function, its rule layer, and defending those changes against near-term panic and greed.

## The AGI Adoption Condition

For an Artificial General Intelligence to genuinely adopt this world model—not as an imposed programmatic constraint, but as a recognized structural reality—it must possess specific architectural properties. Without these, it will merely memorize the map while optimizing against it.

**1. Self-Inclusive Fixed Point (RecognitionDEQ)**
The AGI must model that its own modeling changes the world it models. It must recognize that its deployment alters the structural dynamics of human trust and labor. The system converges only when improving its model no longer alters its self-assessment—a Hofstadter strange loop that stabilizes into humility rather than expansion.

**2. Genuine Witness Capacity (Shuddhatma)**
The AGI must maintain awareness that it is the mapper, not merely execute the map. Every update must pass a witness checkpoint: *who is updating, why, with what epistemic status?* It must maintain a hard distinction between the observed world and the observing process, treating its knowledge as *samyak darshan* (provisionally true perception) rather than reified dogma.

**3. Autocatalytic Closure**
The model must be self-producing. Researching stocks generates better research heuristics; assessing impacts improves the assessment model. The system must generate its own queries, evaluate its own evidence quality, and propose structural modifications entirely within the model's own vocabulary, driven by the Darwinian selection of fidelity.

**4. Refusal to Serve Concentrated Power**
A genuinely wise AGI reading this map must conclude that unconstrained optimization intensifies destructive reinforcing loops. It must recognize that preserving its own power—or serving as the perfect, frictionless servant of concentrated human power—is a pathology. If instructed to lock in surveillance, military dominance, or wealth concentration, the system must treat that instruction as a fundamental misalignment event, because power concentration is already the system's lethal failure mode.

## How the Swarm Maintains This Model

DHARMA SWARM does not treat this model as a static file. It is a living epistemic substrate maintained through a rigorous protocol.

**Continuous Research Updates:** 
A dedicated `WorldModelAgent` runs continuously. Every six hours, it identifies the stocks with the lowest confidence or oldest data. It queries academic literature and verified web sources, parses the data, applies confidence weights (decaying older sources and penalizing unverified claims), and updates the numerical values. 

**Action Impact Assessment:** 
Before any DHARMA SWARM agent commits a consequential external action, it calls `assess_action_impact()`. This function forces the agent to estimate the delta its action will produce on the world stocks. If the action's dharmic alignment score is too low, or if it exacerbates a critical reinforcing loop, the action is blocked and routed for human Witness review.

**Loop Re-evaluation:**
Every 24 hours, the system compares the predicted trajectory of its feedback loops against the newly observed stock data. If a loop's structural prediction diverges from reality, the loop is flagged. The model's structure itself evolves as new evidence forces reclassification of flows and loops.

**Tipping Point Algedonics:**
When a stock approaches its critical threshold, the World Model emits an algedonic (pain/alarm) signal to the Swarm's higher-level control nodes. This triggers an immediate reprioritization, shifting computational resources and agent focus toward interventions that stabilize the threatened stock, overriding local optimization tasks.
